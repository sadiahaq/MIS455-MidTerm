About Paws and Claws

Paws and Claws is a popular pet store located in Bashundhara, Dhaka. It caters to a wide range of pet needs, offering various products and services for pets including dogs, cats, birds, and small animals. The store provides high-quality pet food, grooming supplies, toys, and accessories. Additionally, Paws and Claws is known for its friendly and knowledgeable staff who are always ready to help pet owners with advice on pet care and maintenance. This store is a go-to destination for pet enthusiasts in the Bashundhara area, ensuring pets are well-cared for and happy.
